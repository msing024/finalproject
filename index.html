Use either the keys "A" and "D" or "Left Arrow" and "Right Arrow" to move the pieces left or right.

Use either the "W" key or "Up Arrow" key to rotate the piece clockwise.

Press"S" or "Down Arrow" to have the block drop slowly.

The goal of the game is the score points by using the blocks to completely fill up a row, while ensuring that the blocks do not go overtop the screen.
